Ten Things to Know About Advertising

Ten Things to Know About Advertising tells the story of advertising through examining techniques that have been employed as well as psychological reasons behind how and why it works. The illustrations are styled after the 1930s - 1950s, the period of american history where advertising took off. The writing is written in the perspective of an door to door salesman.
