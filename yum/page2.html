Sailors Lentil Soup

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1⁄2 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1⁄2 tsp salt
  • 3⁄4 cup shortening
  • 1 1⁄2 cups packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 tbs fresh lemon juice
  • 3 tbs lemon zest
  • 1 cup raisins

  1. 1. Preheat oven to 350° F. Lightly grease a 9 inch square baking pan and set aside for future instruction.
  2. 2. Combine flour, baking soda, spices and salt and set aside. In a large bowl, cream together shortening and brown sugar. Beat in the eggs one at a time.
  3. 3. Add in lemon juice and zest. Gradually blend in the dry ingredients. Stir in raisins. Spread batter evenly in the baking pan for consistent lemon bars.
  4. 4. Bake 25–30 minutes until lightly colored on top. Cool in the pan on wire rack. Maybe drizzled with a simple confectioners’ sugar icing. Cut into large or small bars and serve to friends new and old.

Servings 36 Ready In 45 m
